Blog Drafter

A blog drafter app that makes it possible
for a blog writer to make a rough draft, share it with other
blog writers and get feedback on their rough draft before publishing.
If you are a blog writer and writing blogs is your career,
this app makes it to where you can use other blog writers
as editors for your first draft before you release anything to
the public, making your blogs more likely to be read!
